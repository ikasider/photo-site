About me

I’ve been passionate about photography since I was 14 years old. From the very beginning, it fascinated me how a single image can tell a thousand stories. I love capturing beautiful landscapes and the little details that often go unnoticed. But what excites me most is catching genuine, raw emotions that make moments alive forever. When people stop noticing the camera, that’s when the real magic happens — everything feels natural and spontaneous. I live in Haifa, Israel, a place full of stunning views that inspire my work every day. Although I specialize mostly in landscape photography, I also enjoy photographing people and their unique stories. Shooting portraits allows me to connect with my subjects and reveal their true selves. I work with professional cameras and lenses that help me bring my vision to life. Natural light is my favorite — it adds warmth and depth to every photo. But I’m not afraid of challenges; shooting at night or in difficult conditions pushes me to grow as an artist. Photography, for me, is more than just pictures — it’s a way to preserve memories and emotions. Every frame is a chance to see the world differently and share that perspective with others. I strive to create images that speak to the heart and invite viewers to feel what I felt behind the lens. Ultimately, I believe the best photos are the ones that tell honest stories and capture the beauty in everyday life.
